Hey there!

I’m Anastasiia, a UX/UI designer living in Berlin. My journey into design began with a unique twist — I used to be a flight attendant! Through my travels and interactions, I discovered a love for understanding people’s needs and making their experiences better.

I believe in the power of simplicity and practicality, and I’m passionate about blending these elements to create designs that are both user-focused and visually appealing. I also believe that good design should be accessible to everyone, no matter their background or preferences.

I am all about finding inspiration in everyday life, whether it’s through new experiences, trying different cuisines, exploring new destinations or simply getting lost in the city streets. I’m excited to be a part of the ever-evolving design world and to bring my unique perspective to creating meaningful digital experiences.
